Veteran Player Rules

If a player has become a veteran, the following rules apply:

Eligibility:

  • This rule is only applicable for WC Bowlers.
  • If any WC player has played more than 1000 league matches, they will be considered a veteran.

Level Reduction:

Veteran players will start losing their levels as follows:

Star Level Matches to Lose 1 Level
1 Star Player Every 10 league matches
2 Star Player Every 20 league matches
3 Star Player Every 30 league matches
4 Star Player Every 40 league matches

Important Note: Veteran players will only lose their level if they participate in league matches.

How to Avoid Players Becoming Veteran

If you want to prevent your players from reaching veteran status and losing levels, follow these strategies:

  • Rotate Your Squad: Avoid overusing the same WC bowler in league matches. Use a mix of players.
  • Limit League Matches: If a player is nearing 1000 league matches, consider resting them in non-essential matches.
  • Utilize Friendly Matches: Play friendly or practice matches instead of league matches to keep their experience high without aging them.
  • Focus on Training: Prioritize training and player development instead of relying solely on match experience.
  • Manage Workload: Distribute matches evenly among bowlers to prevent any one player from reaching 1000 matches too soon.

By following these strategies, you can prolong your WC players' effectiveness and keep them from becoming veterans too quickly.
