How to Use Elite Academy

The Elite Academy is designed to help you hone your skills. Gain points to upgrade your Hitwicket star and climb the ranks. Below is the points distribution for Hitwicket Star Up Elite:

Basic Points Distribution

Star Type Points
Common 1 point
Reliable 1 Star 3 points
Reliable 2 Star 10 points
Brilliant 1 Star 15 points
Brilliant 2 Star 40 points
Brilliant 3 Star 95 points
WC 1 Star 60 points
WC 2 Star 150 points
WC 3 Star 375 points

Upgrade Combination Examples

  • WC 1 to 2 Star: 75 points (Example: 1 Brilliant 2 Star = 40 points, 3 Reliable 2 Star = 30 points, 1 Reliable 1 Star = 3 points, 2 Common = 2 points)
  • WC 2 to 3 Star: 190 points (Example A: 1 WC 2 Star = 150 points, 1 Brilliant 2 Star = 40 points)
    OR (Example B: 4 Brilliant 2 Star = 160 points, 3 Reliable 2 Star = 30 points)
  • WC 3 to 4 Star: 200 points (Example: 1 WC 2 Star = 150 points, 1 Brilliant 2 Star = 40 points, 1 Reliable 2 Star = 10 points)
    OR try other combinations as needed.
  • Brilliant 1 to 2 Star: 20 points (Example: 2 Reliable 2 Star = 20 points)
  • Brilliant 2 to 3 Star: 60 points (Example A: 1 Brilliant 2 Star = 40 points, 2 Reliable 2 Star = 20 points)
    OR (Example B: 6 Reliable 2 Star = 60 points)
  • Reliable 1 to 2 Star: 5 points (Example: 1 Reliable 1 Star = 3 points, 2 Common = 2 points OR 5 Common = 5 points)

Leverage these points and combinations to maximize your upgrades and stay ahead of the competition. Use your earned points wisely to boost your performance on the field!

How to Use TP

TP (Teleport) can be a game-changer in HitWicket! Follow these tips to maximize your advantage:

  • Understand the cooldown period and plan your moves.
  • Use TP to quickly escape unfavorable situations or surprise opponents.
  • Practice in training mode to master the technique.

Use TP wisely to turn the tide of battle.
