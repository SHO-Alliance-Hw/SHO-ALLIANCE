SHO Reliable Tournament

Get ready for an epic journey with the best teams in cricket!

Tournament Format

Group Stage

42 teams compete in 6 groups, each with 7 teams. The top 16 teams advance.

  • 6 Groups of 7 Teams
  • Top 2 from each group advance
  • 4 Best 3rd-placed teams qualify
Phase 1: Knockouts

16 teams compete in a mini-league format. The top 8 teams will qualify.

  • 4 groups of 4 teams
  • Top 2 teams from each group advance
Transition Phase

The top 8 teams play to determine the final 4 teams advancing to phase 2.

  • Points-based system
  • 4 teams advance to phase 2
